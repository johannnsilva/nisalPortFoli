About Me

Passionate pastry chef dedicated to creating exceptional culinary experiences

As a First Commis Pastry Chef at the prestigious Shangri-La Colombo, I bring creativity, precision, and passion to every dessert I create. With over 5 years of experience in high-end hospitality, I specialize in contemporary pastry techniques while honoring traditional flavors.

My culinary journey has taken me through various renowned establishments, where I've honed my skills in artisanal pastry-making, dessert presentation, and kitchen management. I believe that every dessert should tell a story and create an unforgettable moment.
